Northeastern University Office of Alumni Relations

Holiday Email Header

Working in The Office of Alumni Relations was a very transformative time for me as a designer. I had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects and receive mentorship. The above is an example of something I had the chance to design; it is a holiday email header that accompanied the annual holiday email message distributed by The Office of Alumni Relations at Northeastern University.

Grad Sign

I designed this sign in Illustrator for Northeastern graduates to hold during photoshoots sponsored by The Office of Alumni Relations after commencement. This was one of four sign designs that I came up with.

Rewards Card

This was designed as a thank you card for early participants in the Northeastern Rewards program. The card accompanied a free sweatshirt that participants received and encouraged them to post on social media to help grow awareness for the program.
